about me

Who am i?

Hello, my name is Jason. I am English and have been teaching since 2009. I have a CELTA qualification and a general teaching qualification (CTLLS). I am a member of the College of Teachers and I have a DBS certificate (Criminal Records Check).

Correctconversation.com exists because of my frustration with traditional English lessons. Their focus is mainly on learning lots of words and rules on grammar and pronunciation. They also usually concentrate on reading and writing, with silly, repetitive exercises in expensive textbooks. In addition, when there are speaking and listening activities, these are normally artificial, unreal role plays or fake debates.

I believe that language is best learnt where the learner has a real reason to speak in that language, in a real environment, with a real person who can explain simply anything the learner does not understand.

I also think, and know from my own experience as a language learner, that a learner wants instant correction, so he or she does not repeat the same mistake again.

To end I will ask: did we not learn our own languages in these ways?

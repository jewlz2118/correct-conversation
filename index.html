So, what is 'correct conversation' all about?

It's about speaking for a purpose: having a real conversation - not an artificial role play or fake debate.

It's about communicating with a human being not doing an exercise in an expensive text book.

It's about being in a real everyday environment: like a cafe or coffee shop not a formal classroom, which can be intimidating.

It's about instant correction with a simple explanation - not delayed or involving embarrassment in front of other learners.

Questions?

Why can't I just have a conversation with a friend?

Well, of course, you can. But you may not be lucky enough to have a friend who is a native speaker and qualified teacher, which is what correctconversation.com offers.

How do I know if my English is good enough to take part in a correct conversation?

Well, if you can understand most of the English on this website, then you are good enough to take part. Correctconversation.com will build your vocabulary, grammar, and production and understanding skills.

Why do you say 'the natural way'?

Correctconversation.com follows the way we learn languages as children. We listen and speak for a reason to other human beings in real environments and have things explained to us that we don't understand and are corrected when we get things wrong.

Location

Why does it have to be in a real Location, like a cafe, coffee shop, and so on?

This is because, these environments are real unlike those of the classroom. There are other people around - they are normal, everyday environments.

What areas do you serve?

Anywhere in North London.

Lesson fees

How much does it cost?

The cost is £20 an hour or £30 for a two hour session. But remember this is one-to-one teaching with a qualified teacher at a location near to you.

What about payment?

You pay in cash when you meet your correctconversation.com teacher.
